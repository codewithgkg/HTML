The Top Movie of all time by Gaurav

My best three movies

Titanic

"Titanic" is a 1997 epic romance and disaster film that tells the tragic love story of Jack and Rose aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

The Dark Knight

"The Dark Knight" is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan, where Batman faces off against the anarchic Joker in a battle for Gotham's soul.

3 Idiots

"3 Idiots" is a 2009 Indian comedy-drama that follows three friends' journey through engineering college, exploring themes of friendship, education, and the pursuit of passion.
